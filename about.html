Background

This is not something I would say is important to my identity, but I think it grants some insight into who I am. I have learned to value both health and wellness as well as preventative care. Interests

As for my academic interests, I have had several research opportunities through the Department of Sociology & Anthropology. To begin, I conducted an independent study analyzing the role of third places on TCU's campus. Third places are social spaces where people gather outside of their first place (home) and second place (work). This has been a major area of interest since high school as there is an extreme abudance of third places on a University campus (dining halls, recreation centers, dorm lobbies, study spaces, outdoor spaces, coffee shops), and the abundance of these places almost fade as life progresses in America. Secondly, in partnership with the Office of Student Success I conducted focus groups of first and second year students to understand the social reasons contributing to student retention.
